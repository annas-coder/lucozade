ENERGY SPORT ZERO ALERT SHOP

The sustainability of our packaging, and what happens to it when it's empty, is hugely important to us. We design our packaging for circularity and the introduction of tethered caps is our latest step towards making our drinks more sustainable.

The caps on our bottles have always been recyclable, but these are often discarded and therefore not recycled. This new change means that the cap stays attached to the bottle after opening, reducing the potential for it to be littered. We are committed to making sustainable changes to our packaging to help achieve a circular economy for plastics.

We are transitioning our production lines to tethered caps. During this period, you may see both types in stores as older stock is sold through.

All Lucozade bottles and caps have been 100% recyclable for many years. The move to tethered caps is simply to ensure they stay together to improve the collection and recycling process.

We use sweeteners to provide the same great taste without the sugar content.

While Lucozade Zero does not provide energy from sugar, it is designed to be a refreshing, sugar-free alternative for those who want the Lucozade taste without the calories.

Legally, a product can be called 'Zero' sugar if it contains less than 0.5g of sugar per 100ml. The small amount of calories comes from the other ingredients used to create the flavor.
